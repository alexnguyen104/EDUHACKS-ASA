How our model works and its applications

Stage 1

The Model takes a video as input (can be real-time)

Stage 2

Each frame is processed and eventually shows bounding boxes around detected objects/obstacles using yolov8n model

Stage 3

If the obstacles enter the “protected area”, an instruction is shown in text as well as sound (pyttsx3)

Applications

Utilizing this model helps safeguard blind people about potential obstacles in their daily lives
